Our Mission

What we do

AccessSTEM is a completely free, one-day summer camp designed to make STEM feel exciting, welcoming, and hands-on. Students will take part in design challenges and creative activities that build confidence, curiosity, and problem-solving skills.

Who can Apply?

Children in grades 4-5 who are curious about science, building, and trying new things.

Where are we?

Gabriel Robins Elementary School

When is it?

June 30th, 2026

Activities

Each activity is hands-on, beginner-friendly, and made to keep students interested from start to finish.

Build

Students work in small teams to design, test, and improve creative builds using simple materials.

Create

Kids explore STEM ideas through fun challenges that mix imagination, art, and problem solving.

Share

Participants get to show what they made, celebrate their ideas, and learn from one another.
